ABOUT

National Bridge Demolition is the premier bridge removal contractor in the Southeast, proudly serving civil contractors across South Carolina, North Carolina, Georgia, and Tennessee.

With highly specialized crews and a modern fleet of equipment, we complete complex infrastructure teardowns and bridge removals year-round. Our unwavering commitment to strict safety protocols, time efficiency, and precise schedule adherence has made us one of the most trusted, dependable names in the civil demolition industry.

After years of seeing civil infrastructure projects delayed by inefficient removal processes, we established a company entirely dedicated to expert bridge demolition. We fully recognized the complex regulatory demands that civil contractors face when coordinating with state Departments of Transportation, and we designed our entire operations to eliminate those bottlenecks. Today, National Bridge Demolition operates under an uncompromising set of core values: strict worksite safety, rigorous adherence to your project schedule, and unparalleled on-time performance. We believe in executing the job flawlessly to spec, pricing the project right the first time, and leaving every single job site immaculately clean.

We take pride in our work, and we have numerous satisfied civil contractors, municipal planners, DOT project managers, and heavy infrastructure developers that we are proud to call our dedicated partners.
